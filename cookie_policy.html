Cookie Policy

Our website and certain 3rd-party applications enabled on our website use “cookies” to enhance your experience. These cookies are small text files used to store information such as products in your cart, the preferred language of content, trigger for push notifications, Google analytics tracking information to store your preferences. We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our site.

The cookies stored in your local storage saves you time as they help us remember who you are more efficiently. If you do not wish to accept cookies in connection with TechKnock Digital Services, you will need to delete and block or disable cookies via your browser settings. You can set your browser option so that you will not receive cookies and you can also delete existing cookies from your browser. Please note that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of certain TechKnock Digital Services’ services or notifications.
